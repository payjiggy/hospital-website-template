Services

Excellent Medical Services

Emergency Care

Our Emergency Care unit operates 24/7 to provide immediate medical attention for critical and urgent health conditions. We are equipped to handle accidents, trauma, and sudden illnesses with speed, expertise, and compassion.

Operation & Surgery

Medinet Hospitals offers safe and reliable surgical services performed by experienced surgeons in well-equipped operating theatres. From minor procedures to major surgeries, patient safety and successful recovery are our top priorities.

Outdoor Checkup

Our Outpatient Checkup services allow patients to consult with doctors, receive diagnoses, and access treatment without hospital admission. We ensure efficient care, minimal waiting time, and professional medical attention.

Ambulance Service

We provide prompt and well-equipped ambulance services for emergency response and patient transportation. Our ambulances are staffed with trained personnel to ensure safe and timely medical care during transit.

Medicine & Pharmacy

Our in-house pharmacy provides genuine, high-quality medications prescribed by our doctors. We ensure proper dispensing, patient guidance, and reliable access to essential medicines at all times.

Blood Testing

Our diagnostic laboratory offers accurate and timely blood testing services using modern equipment. We support early diagnosis, treatment monitoring, and preventive healthcare with reliable test results.
